QuickMynt Privacy Policy

 

Effective Date: 12/12/ 2025

 

QuickMynt ("QuickMynt," "we," "us," or "our") is a financial technology product operated by Badmus Holdings LLC ("Company," "Badmus Holdings," or "we"), a U.S. limited liability company. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you access or use the QuickMynt website, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

 

This Privacy Policy is designed to reflect industry-standard practices for U.S.-based earned wage access (EWA) platforms and to satisfy applicable privacy laws, app store requirements, and fintech data‑handling expectations.

 

1. Scope

 

This Privacy Policy applies to:

 

-        Visitors to the QuickMynt website

-        Users of the QuickMynt mobile application

-        Individuals who create an account, connect payroll systems, or request earned wage access

 

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, applications, or services that may be linked through the Services.

 

2. Information We Collect

 

A. Information You Provide Directly

 

When you create an account or use the Services, we may collect:

 

-        Full name

-        Email address

-        Phone number

-        Date of birth

-        Residential address

-        Employer name

-        Payroll provider name

-        Login credentials (stored in encrypted or hashed form)

 

B. Payroll, Employment, and Income Data

 

With your explicit authorization, we collect employment and income data from payroll, income, and time‑and‑attendance systems connected by you, which may include:

 

-        Employer name and employment status

-        Job title and start date

-        Pay frequency and compensation structure

-        Earnings, paystubs, and shift‑level work data

-        Taxes, deductions, and garnishments

 

This data is accessed solely for the purpose of determining eligibility for earned wage access, calculating available earnings, preventing fraud, and operating the Services.

 

C. Financial and Payment Information

 

To facilitate payouts and repayments, we may collect:

 

-        Bank account information

-        Debit card information

-        Payment method tokens and identifiers

 

 

Payment information is processed by third‑party payment processors. QuickMynt does not store full card numbers or online banking credentials.

 

D. Identity Verification Information

 

To comply with legal obligations and reduce fraud, we may collect limited identity data, such as:

 

-        Date of birth

-        Last four digits of a Social Security number

-        Address verification data

 

E. Automatically Collected Information

 

When you access the Services, we may automatically collect:

 

-        IP address

-        Device identifiers

-        Browser type and operating system

-        App interaction data

-        Log files and timestamps.

 

3. How We Use Your Information

 

We use personal information to:

 

-        Create and manage user accounts

-        Verify identity, employment, and income

-        Determine eligibility and calculate available earned wages

-        Process wage access payouts and scheduled repayments

-        Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud or unauthorized activity

-        Communicate with users regarding accounts, transactions, and service updates

-        Improve, secure, and maintain the Services

-        Comply with legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations

 

QuickMynt does not sell personal information.

 

4. Legal Basis for Processing

 

We process personal data based on:

 

-        User consent

-        Performance of contractual obligations

-        Compliance with applicable laws

-        Legitimate business interests, including fraud prevention, security, and service improvement.

 

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

 

We may share information with:

 

A. Service Providers

 

Third‑party service providers that support our operations, including:

 

-        Payroll and income verification providers

-        Payment processors

-        Identity verification and fraud‑prevention services

-        Cloud infrastructure and data‑hosting providers

 

These providers are contractually restricted to using personal information only as necessary to perform services on our behalf.

 

B. Legal and Regulatory Disclosures

 

We may disclose personal information when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

 

C. Business Transactions

 

If Badmus Holdings LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable privacy protections.

 

6. What We Do Not Do

 

For clarity:

 

-        We do not sell personal data

-        We do not share data for advertising or marketing networks

-        We do not access payroll systems without explicit user authorization

-        We do not modify payroll or bank settings without user action or consent.

 

7. Data Retention

 

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

 

-        Provide the Services

-        Meet legal, regulatory, and accounting obligations

-        Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

 

Data that is no longer required is securely deleted or anonymized.

 

8. Data Security

 

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information, including encryption, access controls, and monitoring. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

 

9. User Rights and Choices

 

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

 

-        Access personal information

-        Request corrections

-        Request deletion of certain data

-        Withdraw previously granted consent

 

 

Requests can be submitted using the contact information below.

 

10. U.S. State Privacy Rights

 

Residents of certain U.S. states, including California, Virginia, and Colorado, may have additional rights under state privacy laws. QuickMynt complies with applicable state privacy requirements.

 

11. Childrens Privacy

 

The Services are intended only for individuals aged 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

 

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

 

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through the Services or by other appropriate means.
